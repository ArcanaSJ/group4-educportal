History

When was ISCP founded

Who founded ISCP

On November 24, 2022, ISCP, the first international online college in
the Philippines territory, was founded.

Dr. Sean Joseph C. Arcana founded ISCP together with his team, they
donated over 1000 books to the School.International State College of
the Philippines was officially founded by a vote by the Great and
General Court of the Cebu Colony.

Organization

International State College of the Philippines is an independent and self-governing institution consisting
of the University, its divisions, departments and faculties, and the colleges.

The colleges and halls

The colleges and halls

The colleges and halls

There are 100 colleges and three societies are essential
components of the University, to which they are linked
through a federal system. Each college is independent and self-governing, with a
charter approved by the Privy Council, and is governed
by a Head of House who is directly elected and
appointed by the governing body. The governing body
is made up of a number of Fellows, the majority of
whom also hold University positions.
