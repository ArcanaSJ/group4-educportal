BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION-MAJOR IN MARKETING MGT.

The BSBA course, major in Marketing Management is a program which deals with the nature, scope and role of marketing to make our economy grow and help people attain a decent standard of living. The marketing concepts and specialized activities like marketing research, selling, and product planning and development, etc. are designed to prepare the students to become proactive participants in the lucrative world of business. The program offers Marketing Management as major, and Advertising/PR or Management as minor.


BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) program is a four-year degree program which focuses on the study of computer utilization and computer software to plan, install, customize, operate, manage, administer and maintain information technology infrastructure. It likewise deals with the design and development of computer-based information systems for real-world business solutions.


BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN TOURISM MANAGEMENT

The program is a ladderized curriculum which provides students with specific skills and competencies to enhance their opportunities to work while studying. Students receive a Certificate in Tourism Sales and Marketing upon one year completion, an Associate in Travel and Tour Operation in the second year, a Diploma in Tourism Supervision in the third year and a Degree in Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management upon completion of the program. The Tourism Management Program covers the study of various components related to the travel and tour industry.


BACHELOR OF ARTS IN PSYCHOLOGY

The Bachelor of Science in Psychology is designed to prepare students towards a scientific understanding of human behavior. Students will have the experiences in psychological evaluation, research work and community involvement. It also prepares the student for graduate work
