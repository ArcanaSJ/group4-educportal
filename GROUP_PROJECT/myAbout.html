ABOUT ISCP

International State College of Philippines is at the frontier of academic and intellectual discovery. Those who venture here—to learn, research, teach, work, and grow—join nearly four centuries of students and scholars in the pursuit of truth, knowledge, and a better world.

MISSION

"The International State College of the Philippines provides a low-cost, high-quality education that meets the needs of both the local and international communities."

VISION

"Demonize high-quality education." Be the industry visionary and leader. Give hope and change lives."

HISTORY

When was ISCP founded?

On November 24, 2022, ISCP, the first international online college in the Philippines territory, was founded.

Who founded ISCP?

Dr. Sean Joseph C. Arcana founded ISCP together with his team,they donated over 1000 books to the School.International State College of the Philippines was officially founded by a vote by the Great and General Court of the Cebu Colony.

ORGANIZATION

International State College of the Philippines is an independent and self-governing institution consisting of the University, its divisions, departments and faculties, and the colleges.

The colleges and halls

The colleges and halls

The colleges and halls

There are 100 colleges and three societies are essential components of the University, to which they are linked through a federal system.
Each college is independent and self-governing, with a charter approved by the Privy Council, and is governed by a Head of House who is directly elected and appointed by the governing body. The governing body is made up of a number of Fellows, the majority of whom also hold University positions.
